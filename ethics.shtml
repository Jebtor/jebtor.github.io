Kickstarter page for Ethics course

For a course called 'responsibilities' aka 'Ethics for CreaTe' we had to design a Kickstarter page for an imaginary product. This product should attempt to address an ethical/societal/political problem of some sort and contribute to the user's well being. It is also important to back your claims by empirical support, guidelines and/or codes of ethics. The result of my assignment can be found here.
