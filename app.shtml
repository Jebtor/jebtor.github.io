Interactive Application
22nd of January 2015
Together with BIT and computer science students, I made an interactive Android application that can be played by groups over the course of a couple of days.

The application could be played as an introductory activity. It should make it easier for a new group of people to get to know each other. Each player gets assigned a target. This target is another player in the group. A player needs to find their target, and try to 'kill' him or her. In the game you can 'kill' someone by being alone with them. The game can help by providing participants with common-ground topics - topic you both like.
