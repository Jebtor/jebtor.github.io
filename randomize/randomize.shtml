The RANDOM game
10th of September 2017

For prototype II we had to design a game that can be spectated, has a digital component, is for two players and either infinite play or rounds of <2 minutes. We came up with RANDOMIZE, a game to socialize or just to do something crazy, to amuse on a night out or at a party. The game can be played anywhere with anyone. In the game two players are randomly selected from the spectators. Each player is given a random sentence. Both players have to express these sentences, no excuses. Imagination solves every situation. After both players depicted their words the crowd choses a winner. The winner gets to add new words to the game. Slides for our presentation can be found here.
