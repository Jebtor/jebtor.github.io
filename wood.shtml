Wood Working
4th of June 2014
The university offered a wood working course for the students that were interested. I signed up for the course and spent 8 afternoons in the workshop. The course was about both hand and electric tools and in the end we got to make our own piece. I decided I wanted to make something similar to the logo shown below.
Below you can see how far I have gotten with the piece. I plan to finish the work somewhere in the summer. When it is finished it should resemble an interpretation of Odin with his two ravens, Huginn and Muninn.
