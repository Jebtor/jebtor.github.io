SpookyBall
24th of September 2017
For prototype III we had to adjust an existing game. We decided to adjust brick out, normally in brick out you have to clear the level by hitting all the blocks with your ball. In our game, however, a new block is spawned when you hit one with your ball. Blocks only disappear when the whole row is filled with blocks, similiar to Tetris.
