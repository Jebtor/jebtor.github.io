Tour Guide 3.0

Commissioned by techniekmuseum HEIM in Hengelo, me and some fellow students made a tour guide robot for the robotics section which will open in 2014. We made a prototype robot tour guide which is supposed to lead visitors around in the new robotics section. During the tour you have to help and play games with the robot. If you progress through the tour the robot will start to learn and evolve in a smarter robot. Below I have posted the photos of the prototype.
