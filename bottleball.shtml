Bottle Ball
3rd of September 2017
For the course Game Prototyping at ITU our group was given the task to design a sport that made use of a prop.

The sport has to be team based, with teams consisting of at maximum three players. Furthermore, the sport has to make use of a prop and contain rounds that can take up to a maximum of 10 minutes. Our group came up with and playtested Bottleball. A full description of Bottleball can be found here .

An interesting aspect of the game, in my opinion, is the fact that it uses a water bottle to keep track of score. This creates a more continuous form of points as opposed to counting goals.
